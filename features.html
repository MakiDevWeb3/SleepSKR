Features

What SleepSkr does

SleepSkr is intentionally focused. It tracks nights, helps users stay consistent, and keeps the experience calm and readable.

01

Sleep logging

Track bedtime, wake time, duration, streaks, and private notes from a simple Android interface.

02

Gentle reminders

Night and morning reminders help users stay consistent without noisy onboarding or ads.

03

Local data

Sleep data stays on the device. The app is built around local storage rather than cloud accounts.

Core design

Calm and readable

  • Soft palette Beige and black keep the app quiet and easy on the eyes.
  • Simple flow The interface stays close to the task instead of adding extra screens.
  • No login wall Users can open the app and use the core features immediately.

Privacy-first

What it avoids

  • No ads No advertising banners or ad SDKs are included.
  • No tracking No analytics or surveillance-style SDKs are used.
  • No private storage by default The app keeps user data local and simple.
