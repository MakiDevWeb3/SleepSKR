SleepSkr terms

SleepSkr is a sleep logging and reminder app for Android. It is not medical software, and any wallet or donation flow is optional and user-approved.

App description

What SleepSkr is

SleepSkr helps users track sleep sessions, reminders, and notes on Android. The app keeps data local on the device and offers optional wallet support for users who want it.

Medical disclaimer

No medical advice

SleepSkr does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a personal tracking tool only. Users should not rely on the app for medical decisions and should contact a qualified professional if they have sleep or health concerns.

Wallet and donations

Blockchain and donation disclaimer

Wallet use is optional. SleepSkr never needs your private key or recovery phrase. If you connect a wallet, the wallet app stays in control of signing and broadcasting any transaction.

Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Any donation or support transfer is a real blockchain transaction. Once it is broadcast and confirmed, it cannot be undone by SleepSkr or by the publisher.
