Privacy by design
Your data stays
on your phone.

Zero data collection

No analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry. Nothing is ever sent anywhere. Your sleep data is yours alone.

Local storage only

All data lives in a Room Database on your device. No internet required. Works fully offline. Uninstall = data gone.

Wallet is optional

Solana Mobile wallet is 100% opt-in. Reads your public address only. If you choose to donate SOL, that is a real Mainnet transaction you review and approve inside your wallet — never automatic, never hidden.
